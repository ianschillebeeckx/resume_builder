# Senior Manager, Data & ML (Revenue & Growth)
**Company:** GrowthTech
**Location:** San Francisco, CA (Hybrid, 3 days/week)
**Salary:** $268,000 - $315,000 OTE/Base

---

## About the Role

Lead two connected missions inside GrowthTech's Data & ML organization:
1. Own quantitative backbone of GrowthTech's revenue and financial data
2. Accelerate growth on self-serve SMB business through data science and experimentation

Revenue side: forecasting, revenue, cost data models — source of truth for upsell, churn, unit economics, pricing, packaging, strategic finance.

Growth side: PLG data science for SMB — activation, conversion, expansion.

Part of Data & ML leadership team. Shape GrowthTech's AI strategy including agentic fleet deployment and semantic layer.

---

## Responsibilities

- Build and run the team: hire, develop, retain high-performing DS and analysts across Revenue and Growth
- Own revenue data layer: forecasting models, cost/billing data products, reconciliation pipelines, exec dashboards for C-staff and board
- Accelerate SMB growth: PLG data science, experimentation, activation/conversion analytics, pricing optimization, propensity models
- Partner with Finance and Growth leadership; quantitative thought leader for CFO org and Product
- Ship AI-powered workflows: automate analytical pipelines, agentic workflows, reusable skills/context for GrowthTech's agentic platform
- Raise technical bar: modeling rigor, data quality, pipeline reliability, production-readiness

- 3+ years direct people management
- Deep SQL and Python; modern ELT (dbt, Snowflake or equivalent); ML/forecasting methods

- Strong executive communication (C-level)

- SF-based, hybrid 3 days/week
