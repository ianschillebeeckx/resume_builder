# Senior Director of Data Science, Clinical Performance
**Company:** HealthTech Co (Value-Based Care Platform)
**Location:** Remote-friendly (startup, founded 2020)
**Salary:** $210,000–$250,000 + bonus + equity
**Reports to:** Chief Data Science Officer

---

## About the Company

HealthTech Co empowers primary care providers, health systems, and physician-led networks to succeed in value-based care. Platform delivers technology, financial tools, and expert services for Medicare patients. Founded 2020, backed by top-tier VCs. Thousands of providers across 44 states.

---

## About the Role

Primary quantitative architect of systems that power and measure clinical impact. Define rigorous frameworks that prove transformative value of the company's enablement of patient care. Own and execute multi-year research roadmap. Establish technical "source of truth" — definitive narrative on how clinical interventions translate into population health outcomes and financial sustainability. Guide company strategy, shape product evolution, cornerstone of relationships with health systems.

Intersection of deep technical expertise and executive influence. Comfortable leading through complex causal inference study or risk-adjustment model and standing before C-suite to translate findings into strategic vision.

---

## Responsibilities

- Own the Scientific Methodology: Define and direct research frameworks and model architectures for clinical risk identification and value attribution. Establish technical systems that rigorously calculate clinical impact.
- Strategic Roadmap & Long-term Planning: Architect multi-year roadmap for research into clinical performance anticipating evolving needs of value-based care and Medicare Advantage.
- Executive Partnership & Influence: Primary consultant for majority of management team, using data-driven insights to influence product direction, operational strategy, enterprise-level decision making.
- Narrative Building & Publishing: Translate complex technical study designs and statistical findings into clear, compelling strategic narratives. Present at industry conferences and author peer-reviewed journal publications.
- Team Leadership & Mentorship: Grow and empower elite team of data scientists, fostering culture of high trust, continuous feedback, and technical excellence.
- Cross-Functional Integration: Drive enhancement of predictive product capabilities, increasing precision and explainability of clinical models for actionable user insights.
