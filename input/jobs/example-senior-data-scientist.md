# Business Data Science
**Company:** AI Platform Co
**Location:** San Francisco, CA (Hybrid, 3 days/week)
**Salary:** $255,000 - $405,000

---

## About the Team

Business Data Science team uses data and analytics to optimize business performance, drive growth, and foster meaningful partnerships. Partners with Sales (GTM), Marketing, Partnerships, Support, Finance, Product, and Growth.

---

## About the Role

Build data-driven culture around insight generation, decision making, and strategy. Focused on driving customer success within the company's business products. Projects include customer lifecycle interventions for activation & onboarding, target audiences for feature launches, measuring efficacy of emails/events/interventions for engagement.

---

## Responsibilities

- Embed with Customer Success as trusted partner, driving customer adoption and engagement
- Establish key metrics, run experiments, perform analysis to understand incrementality of efforts
- Proactively surface insights and opportunities to drive engagement and growth
- Build tools and systems for stakeholders to self-serve routine data and insights
- Become expert in the company's data and systems; self-serve all underlying business data
- Partner with other data scientists to share knowledge and synthesize learnings

---

## Qualifications

- 7+ years in Data Science roles within dynamic, outcome-driven organizations
- Expertise in statistics and causal inference (experimentation and observational)
- Proficiency in Python and R
- Expertise in SQL, large datasets, ETL workflows
- Experience with BI tools (Mode, Tableau, Looker)
- Strategic and impact-driven mindset
- Strong stakeholder communication and partnership skills
- Data storytelling via decks, memos, dashboards
- Track record in Finance, Marketing, Partnerships, Sales, Support, or other GTM areas
