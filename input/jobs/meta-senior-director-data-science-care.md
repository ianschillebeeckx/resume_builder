# Senior Director, Data Science, Care

**Source:** LinkedIn
**Company:** Meta
**Location:** NORAM (US-based)
**Compensation:** $314,000 - $361,000 + bonus + equity + benefits

## About The Role

Lead the Care Data Science team. Responsible for developing and executing a comprehensive data science strategy that drives customer satisfaction, optimizes operational efficiency, and will help retain millions of users and billions in revenue. Work closely with cross-functional teams to identify opportunities, design experiments, and help develop AI and automation solutions. Partner with cross-functional teams on building out cutting edge agentic capabilities to scale customer support.

Team is split between EMEA and NORAM, with larger presence in EMEA. Looking for a NORAM-based leader to help navigate partnerships with primarily US-based partner teams (Monetization, Facebook, Instagram, AI Automation and Solutions) and executive presentations to the COO and CEO.

Building out cutting edge solutions that rely on the latest LLM/agentic technologies to scale solutions to billions of people, including advertisers and creators around the world. These efforts are part of the top 5 Meta priority areas.

### Leadership
- Lead a team of ~50 data scientists, including 1 Director

- Develop and execute a comprehensive data science strategy that aligns with the org's overall goals and objectives

- Drive customer satisfaction by identifying opportunities, designing experiments, and enabling development of AI and automation solutions

- Work closely with cross-functional teams, including product management, engineering, and operations

### Communication
- Communicate complex technical concepts to non-technical stakeholders, including executives, product managers, and engineers
